Cancer

Cancer causes cells to divide uncontrollably. This can result in tumors, damage to the immune system, and other impairment that can be fatal

Weight Loss

People with obesity have a higher risk of various health complications. For this reason, many people would like to lose weight. Ways of losing weight can include dietary changes, exercising, using supplements, and setting realistic targets.

Diabates

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs either when the pancreas does not produce enough insulin or when the body cannot effectively use the insulin it produces.
